This page shows the experimental information supplied along with primary data (e.g. sequencing reads) for analysis. This information may have been used to apply a batch correction and to define groups of samples to compare against one another. Factors from this table can be used to color plots in other parts of the application.

#### Category counts

The 'Category counts' tab lets you count samples by a categorical (non-numeric) annotation field, for example the number of samples in each treatment group. Choose a second field under 'Split by' to break each category down further (e.g. counts per group split by batch), shown as grouped or stacked bars. Identifier-like fields (e.g. a sample ID or name), where almost every row has its own distinct value, aren't offered, since there'd be little to count beyond '1' per row.
